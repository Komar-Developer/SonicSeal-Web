Frequently asked questions

How does SonicSeal protect my tracks?

SonicSeal embeds an inaudible 32-bit forensic watermark (FFT-domain, 15–18 kHz band) into every audio file you forge. The mark survives MP3 re-encoding, EQ, time-stretch and DJ-software ripping, and decodes back to a unique buyer ID even from a 30-second clip.

Is my audio uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything happens locally on your Mac. The app does not make any external HTTP requests for its core functionality (forge / decode / playback). See our Privacy Policy for details.

What does the 14-day trial include?

Full Forge + Booth + Forensic functionality, capped to 3 minutes of audio per track and with an inaudible "TRIAL" canary watermark. The trial counter shows in the macOS Dock during the last 5 days.

How do I activate a license?

Launch SonicSeal → the activation screen appears. Paste your license key (use the ⎘ button or right-click → Paste) and click Activate. The license binds to this Mac and stays valid offline.

Can I move my license to another Mac?

Yes. Open SonicSeal on the new Mac, enter the same key, click Activate. Each license supports up to 3 simultaneous Macs. If you exceed the cap, the oldest binding is revoked automatically.

Where are my forged files saved?

By default to ~/Music/SonicSeal/. Change it from File → Output Folder… (⇧⌘O).

Why does SonicSeal need network.client / network.server entitlements?

The app runs a local FastAPI sidecar bound to 127.0.0.1 (loopback only) so the AES master secret stays out of the renderer process. No external connections are made. See the Privacy Policy for a full breakdown.

Will SonicSeal work without an internet connection?

Yes, 100%. The only network activity is one-time license activation and optional auto-update checks (Gumroad build only).

I lost my license key — what now?

Open a support ticket above with your Gumroad order ID (or Mac App Store receipt) and we'll re-issue the key.

I'm a record label — can I get a multi-seat license?

Yes. Open a ticket with subject "Label license" and we'll set you up with the Producer plan (unlimited seats, custom branding, white-label PDF forensic reports).

System requirements

  • Intel or Apple Silicon (universal binary)
  • ~500 MB free disk space (the app bundles a self-contained Python + ffmpeg)
